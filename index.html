What Is Air Duct Cleaning?

Air duct cleaning involves deep removal of dust, mold spores, pet dander, pollen, and debris from your ductwork—including supply and return ducts, registers, diffusers, and grills. We use high-powered HEPA vacuum systems, contact brushes, and negative-pressure source removal techniques to fully clean your ventilation system without spreading contaminants in your home.
